Current Changes:

--- General ---
-Minigames have been removed. The following charms now have a +1 to max skill levels: Optical Detector, Idol of Vanity, Eternal Bone Pile
-Den of Evil size on Terror and Destruction reduced to same size as Hatred
-Special Box has been removed.
-Mercenary full equip feature implementation now requires having Basemod installed. This also means that Mercenaries may now equip one ring in addition to everything else.

--- Cube Recipes ---
-Cow Portal Tome Recipe no longer requires a Tome of Town Portal
-Affix Burn Recipe now returns a Superior quality item
-Removed Dogmas dropped by Act Bosses. Signets of Skill are now created using a Terror Fauztinville Brain + Arcane Crystal
-Trader's Chest upgrade will no longer destroy the items if conditions aren't met
-Unique uptier and unique item creation recipes removed
-Affix Burn Recipe is now Weapon/Armor + Oil of Absolution.
-Slain Monsters RIP now uses Oil of Absolution
-Item destruction recipes removed
-[UNTESTED] Zomghelp recipe removed, added a recipe to change any Superior/Magic/Rare item to ilvl 1: Item + Oil of Renewal + Oil of Absolution

--- Items ---
-Gemwords have been removed from the game as they're quite useless.
-Testament of the Apes set has been enabled.
-Blink on Pax Mystica changed to oskill
-Goodberries and Symbols of Esu are now sold by Ormus and Jamella, respectively. The recipe for creating Goodberries has been removed.
-Oil of Blessed Luck, previously obtained from the Special Box, is now obtained via Oil of Luck + Arcane Crystal
-Catalyst of Destruction removed
-Removed item tiers 2,3,5
-Removed the following useless Mystic Orbs: Crafting Points (Jewelry), Experience (Weapon), Energy Factor (Weapon), Regenerate Mana (Jewelry)

--- Monsters ---
-Added a new monster - Haunt. This monster replaces Necromorbs in Duncraig. Haunts are significantly tougher than Slain Souls and have an Area of Effect attack

--- Uberquests ---
-Ennead Necromancers no longer cast Miasma
-Removed Lantern Archons from Khalimgrad

--- Technical ---
-All Median strings are now stored in expansionstring.tbl
-Started cleaning up .txts and .tbls.
